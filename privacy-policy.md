# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Welcome to ConvoQuest. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Data We Collect and How We Use It

- Voice and Audio Data: ConvoQuest uses your device's native speech recognition capabilities to transcribe your voice into text. We do not record, store, or transmit your actual audio files to our servers. All audio processing happens locally on your device.

- Conversation Transcripts: To provide you with AI coaching and feedback, the text transcriptions of your practice sessions are sent securely to our third-party artificial intelligence provider (Google Gemini API). These transcripts are processed solely to generate your coach's responses and feedback and are not used to identify you personally.

- Device and Usage Data: We collect basic analytics and crash reports to improve app performance. We also use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) to serve personalized advertisements, provided you have explicitly granted us permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt.

## Data Storage

Your user profile, progress, scores and conversation history are saved locally on your device. If you delete the app or clear your app data, this information will be permanently lost. We do not maintain a central database of your personal conversation history.

## Third-Party Services

We utilize the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

- Google Gemini API: For processing text transcripts and generating AI responses.

- Google AdMob: For serving rewarded advertisements.

- Apple: For securely processing in-app purchases and subscriptions.

## Advertising and Tracking

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to personalize ads and analyze performance. You can control these tracking preferences at any time through your device's privacy settings.
