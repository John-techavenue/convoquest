# About Us

ConvoQuest is your private, judgment-free training ground for real-world conversations.

Whether you freeze up in high-stakes job interviews, struggle with small talk at parties, or want to create natural chemistry on dates, ConvoQuest gives you the tools to practice before it matters.

## Features

- 🗣️ Voice-Based Roleplay: Talk to our virtual coaches (Alex, Sam, and Casey) in realistic, dynamic scenarios.
- 📊 Smart Feedback: Get instant, supportive breakdowns of your Confidence, Fluency, and Charm.
- 🎯 Targeted Drills: Turn your weaknesses into strengths with fun, low-stakes practice sessions.

In ConvoQuest, mistakes are just a way to level up. Confidence isn't a personality trait—it's a trained skill. Let's get practicing! 🚀
